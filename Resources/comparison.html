Comparing and Contrasting Euthanasia

I am comparing the pros and cons of legalizing the practice of Euthanasia. Euthanasia is the official word for "mercy killing", and the practice is currently illegal in 45 states in the United States.

Pros & Cons

    Pros

  • Ends the suffering of the patient
  • Provides dignity to the patient by giving them control of their own life
  • Saves money for the family and the hospital
  • Respects the human right that all humans have the right to life, and death

    Cons

  • Devalues the lives of humans
  • Disrespects rules of certain religions
